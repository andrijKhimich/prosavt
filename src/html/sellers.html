ProSavr Advanced Features Give You Everything You Need

Automated Order Reconciliation

This is an optional setting that automatically adjusts the rebate amount on your product so you’re never spending more than you have to in order to get sales. If the product is selling out fast, it will lower the rebate amount for you, saving your money. If the product is selling too slow, it will increase the rebate for you, getting you more sales, faster.

Automated Order Reconciliation

You just upload your sales report from Amazon and we automatically check our system to verify buyers and the validity of the order numbers that they provide for us. This is a fancy way of saying that we make sure every rebate that goes out is 100% verified - and we do it all for you automatically.
